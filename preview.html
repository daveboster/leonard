Road Trip Leonard

  • For plans, packing lists, and "we leave in ten minutes" energy.
  • Works as a cheerful announcement, countdown, or vacation reply.
  • The sign can change later for other trips, years, or family jokes.

Campfire Leonard

  • For cozy nights, marshmallow negotiations, and emotional decompression.
  • Good for "I'm here," "take a breath," and "snack support is available."
  • Could anchor a calmer set alongside more expressive Leonard poses.
