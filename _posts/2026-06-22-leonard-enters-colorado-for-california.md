On Monday, June 22, Leonard entered Colorado on the long road to California.
This was not the final destination. It was more like the part of the trip where
the road starts acting serious.

The mountains showed up outside the window, the sky opened into layers, and
Leonard kept his usual position near the glass: quiet, watchful, and entirely
ready to provide emotional support at highway speed.

![Leonard entering Colorado on the way to California]({{ '/assets/images/leonard-enters-colorado-california-road-trip.jpg' | relative_url }})

Nobody asked Leonard to navigate. Nobody asked him to choose the playlist. His
assignment was simpler and more important: confirm that the westbound trip had
become real.

By the time Colorado filled the window, Leonard had already accepted the next
chapter. California could wait a little longer. For now, there were mountains,
clouds, and a small fry doing steady work from the back seat.
