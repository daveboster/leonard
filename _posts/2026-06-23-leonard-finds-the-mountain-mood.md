The closer Leonard gets to Colorado, the more serious he becomes about his job:
protect the mood, encourage snack breaks, and make sure the trip keeps feeling
like an adventure.

![Leonard on the way to Colorado]({{ '/assets/images/leonard-heading-to-colorado-2.jpg' | relative_url }})

He is not driving. He is not navigating. He is simply present, which is exactly
what an emotional support fry is supposed to do.
