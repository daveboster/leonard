Leonard has crossed into Colorado, which means the trip has officially shifted
from "on the way" to "we made it to the mountains."

![Leonard crossing into Colorado]({{ '/assets/images/leonard-crossing-into-colorado.jpg' | relative_url }})

The sky opened up, the road tucked into the hills, and Leonard kept watch from
the window like this was exactly the kind of moment he had been preparing for.
He is still a little fry, but Colorado makes even little things feel cinematic.
