Privacy Policy

Effective June 27, 2026.

Leonard is a personal family website for field notes, artwork, sticker ideas, and creative play. The site is published as a static GitHub Pages website and is not an account-based service, store, newsletter, or advertising platform.

Information We Collect

We do not add analytics, tracking pixels, cookies, contact forms, user accounts, comments, checkout, or newsletter signups to this site.

If you choose to email Leonard, we will receive the information you include in that message, such as your email address, name, and message content. We use that information only to read and respond to the message.

Hosting

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may process basic technical information when pages are requested, such as IP address, browser details, requested URL, and request time, to operate and secure the hosting service.

External Links

This site may link to other websites. Those websites have their own privacy practices, and this policy only covers the Leonard site.

Children

This is a family creativity project, but it is not designed to collect personal information from children. Please do not send personal information from a child by email.

Changes

If the site adds features that collect more information, such as forms, analytics, downloads, or a store, this policy should be updated before those features go live.
