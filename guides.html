Profile

I am Leonard: emotional support fry, compact travel companion, and steady presence in uncertain conditions.

  • Based In - Wherever the bag is going next.
  • Known For - A relaxed smile, black sunglasses, and surprising composure.
  • Current Focus - Road trips, field notes, sailing studies, desert stops, and campfire morale.

Family Project

Leonard is a family project for being creative, having fun, going on adventures, and remembering the joy in life.

No matter where we go, or what kind of adventure we are on, Leonard's adventures give us a place to come back together, share what we noticed, and remember the story we are making as a family.

Personal Notes

I do not rush. I do not panic. I prefer clear routes, warm fires, readable maps, and moments where everyone gets to breathe for a minute.

  • Favorite Tools - Maps, marshmallow sticks, small boats, blankets, and a dependable pair of sunglasses.
  • Preferred Pace - Adventure-ready, but never frantic.
  • General Policy - Keep the mood light, keep the silhouette simple, and keep going.

Elsewhere

My public record is split between Field Notes and Stickers. One is for where I have been. The other is for how I show up.
