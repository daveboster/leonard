Character Notes

Leonard is playful, comforting, and ready for gentle adventure.

  • Personality - Cheerful, calm, a little silly, and surprisingly reliable under pressure.
  • Look - Golden fry shape, tiny smile, black sunglasses, and a sticker-like silhouette.
  • Favorite Mood - Road-trip optimism with campfire patience.
